## Favorite Existing Apps - Categorize and Evaluate
### UTD Mobile App
   - **Category:** Utility
   - **Mobile:** Mobile only
   - **Story:** Allows users to get important info about campus and allows students to access certain services
   - **Market:** UT Dallas Students
   - **Habit:** Users can use it throughout the week to access certain fatures that the need at the time.
   - **Scope:** Its scope is very narrow to UTD students and the students who need to access the services on the fly with their phone. 

### Flappy Bird
   - **Category:** Game 
   - **Mobile:** Game is mobile only
   - **Story:** Keeps user entertained on subway rides or car trips
   - **Market:** Anyone with free time 
   - **Habit:** Users can play while commuting to work/school. Forms a habit.
   - **Scope:** All ages, was once the top downloaded game on the app store and inspired many copycat games

### Snapchat
   - **Category:** Social Media 
   - **Mobile:** Avaiable on mobile
   - **Story:** Allows users to share their lives in pictures to their friends
   - **Market:** Anyone who likes to show that they are having fun and takes pictures could enjoy this app. Ability to  engage with their friends.
   - **Habit:** Users can send photos and chat with their friends throughout the day many times. Features like "Stories" encourage more candid posting as well. Users can explore endless pictures in any category imaginable whenever they want. Very habbit forming!
   - **Scope:** Snapchat started out extremely narrow focused. Only the people that you send the photos to can view it. 
 
### Quizlet
- **Category:** Education
- **Mobile:** Available on both mobile and web.
- **Story:** Users can practice and master concepts they are studying using flashcards, fill in the blank, or quiz formats.
- **Market:** Anyone who is strugglin' to learn
- **Habit:** whenever u about to fail
- **Scope:** Anyone who needs to re-enforce concepts while studying.

## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize
1. WerkButty
   - **Description**: App to help find someone to do workouts with. 
   - **Category:** Social Networking
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for quickly getting a buddy to workout with.
   - **Story:** Creates a community of people who like to workout with eachother
   - **Market:** Anyone who's lonely and needs a friend
   - **Habit:** Many young students think about working out but always looking for partners to workout with.
   - **Scope:** Directed towards all ages mainly young students and people who do not have a regular work out buddy.

2. Instalocation
   - **Description**: An app that finds people's instagram accounts at a specific location 
   - **Category:** Social Networking
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for pairing up individuals in the area to improve their feed and share new feed ideas.
   - **Story:** Creates and connects a community of people who like to take pictures and have similar interests.
   - **Market:** Anyone who needs help finding people of similar followings on Instagram.
   - **Habit:** People who use instagram and go out a lot
   - **Scope:** All ages and people who need a group of people to hang with. 

3. FeedMe 
   - **Description**: An app that can take all the ingredients in your fridge and lets you know what dishes you can make
   - **Category:** Utility
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for quickly finding a recipe when youre at your fridge
   - **Story:** People need to eat but are too poor to have a bunch of cookbooks
   - **Market:** Anyone who needs help figuring out what they need
   - **Habit:** Many young students don't know what ingredients to use to cook their favorite meals and are too lazy to go check what ingredients they have in the fridge
   - **Scope:** Directed towards anybody who cooks and wants new recipes or don't know any recipes
